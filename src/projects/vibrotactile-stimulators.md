![vibrotactile stimulator banner](@images/vibe-banner.jpg)

This was a quick project for a labmate who wanted haptic feedback on both arms for a neuroscience experiment. I designed the system to drive the haptic motors with audio signals so that my labmate could easily deliver stimulation from their existing MATLAB experiment code.

- **minimal design** with off-the-shelf parts and a single 3D printed case
- **haptic stimulation** is delivered through a standard audio amp and can be driven by any audio source
- **stereo audio** guarantees accurate timing of stimulation between arms
